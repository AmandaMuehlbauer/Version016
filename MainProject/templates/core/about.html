About Jidder

Welcome to our website! Jidder is an experimental project that aims to make it easier to find independent blogs from amateur content creators.


Jidder is for the curious.


At Jidder we have implemented a fuzzy search algorithm. The goal is to make a tool that is more conducive to exploration and finding random, new things. Think of it like an old-fashioned card catalog.


This is a user-curated community. We encourage users to recommend blogs they find interesting and tag your recommendations by subject.

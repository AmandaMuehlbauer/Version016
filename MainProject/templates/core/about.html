About the WilderNet

Welcome to the WilderNet! The WilderNet is an experimental project that aims to make it easier to find independent blogs from amateur content creators.

The WilderNet is for people who are looking for an internet experience that is fun and serendipitious. Think of it like an old-fashioned card catalog.

This is a user-curated community. We encourage users to recommend blogs they find interesting and tag your recommendations by subject.

Are you the owner of a blog? Add it to our catalog! We do not copy your content or present it as our own.
